Alice's Admin Page

Alice can use her admin page to generate passwords which she then sends to Bob and Carol. The sending of the passwords is not handled by this page. It is assumed to occur outside the scope of this DApp.
Alice can use this page to store the one-time passwords as valid hashed passwords in the contract.
Alice can also use this page to initiate a transfer of money to Bob, which will only be complete after he and Carol provide the correct passwords using Carol's page.
After both Bob and Carol provide the correct password on Carol's page, the ether Alice transferred will be remitted to Carol. Carol will give Bob local currency.
Carol giving Bob local currency is also assumed to take place outside the scope of this DApp.
