Sender's Page

A sender can use this page to initiate a transfer of money to any recipient. The sender generates a one-time password and provides it to the receipient. The transfer is completed when the recipient goes to a registered Exchange Shop and provides his or her password. The exchange shop proprietor withdraws funds for a specific transfer. The recipient must provide his/her password in order for this to work.
The exchange shop proprietor giving the recipent local currency is assumed to take place out of the scope of this DApp.
The initiator of the transfer can also cancel it after it has expired by only providing the receipient password.
