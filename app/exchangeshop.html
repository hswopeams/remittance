Exchange Shop

A sender can use this page to initiate a transfer of money to any recipient. The transfer is completed when the recipient goes to a registered Exchange Shop and provides his or her password. The exchange shop proprietor withdraws funds. The recipient must provide his/her password in order for this to work.
The exchange shop proprietor giving the recipent local currency is assumed to take place out of the scope of this DApp.
